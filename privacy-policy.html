Privacy Policy

App: Lotusbrush
Package: com.lotusbrush.studio
Effective date: June 25, 2026
Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lotusbrush ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our Android application. Lotusbrush is an offline nature coloring app. We designed the app to work without collecting personal data.

1. Summary

  • We do not collect personal information.
  • We do not require an account or login.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs.
  • Core app features work fully offline.
  • Your artwork, notes, and preferences stay on your device.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Lotusbrush does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or postal address
  • Account credentials
  • Precise or approximate location
  • Contacts, SMS, call logs, or phone identifiers
  • Photos or files from outside the app unless you explicitly export artwork through the system gallery
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or crash-reporting data sent to our servers

We do not operate backend servers to receive user data from the app.

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide app functionality, Lotusbrush stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Coloring session progress and saved artwork
  • Favorites and gallery metadata
  • Optional journal notes you create
  • App settings such as theme, sound, haptics, and auto-save preferences
  • Onboarding and daily-pick local preferences

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

4. Permissions and Device Access

Based on the current release manifest, Lotusbrush does not request dangerous Android permissions.

The app does not request:

  • Internet or network access
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Location access
  • Contacts, phone state, or account access
  • Read or write external storage permissions
  • Read media images, video, or audio permissions
  • Notifications permission
  • Query all packages permission
  • Advertising ID permission

When you export artwork, the app saves images to your device gallery using the Android system MediaStore API. This action is initiated by you and saves files only where you choose.

5. Offline Operation

Lotusbrush is designed to work offline. The app does not require an internet connection for browsing artwork, coloring, saving progress, using the local gallery, or changing settings.

6. Third-Party Services

The current version of Lotusbrush does not include third-party advertising, analytics, social login, cloud sync, or tracking SDKs.

If you choose to share exported artwork using Android's system share sheet, that action is handled by apps you select on your device. We do not control those third-party apps.

7. Children's Privacy

Lotusbrush is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13 as a primary audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Because we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no remote account data for us to retain. Data stored locally on your device can be removed by:

  • Deleting individual artwork or notes inside the app
  • Using the in-app "Clear all data" option in Settings
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

9. Security

We take a privacy-first approach by minimizing data collection and keeping user content on the device. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, but Lotusbrush is designed to avoid transmitting your personal content to us.

10. International Users

Because Lotusbrush does not collect personal information, no cross-border transfer of personal data by us occurs.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes may also be reflected in app updates or store listing notes where appropriate.
